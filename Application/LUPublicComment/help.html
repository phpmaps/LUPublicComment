Online Help:

This Land Use Public Comment application provides the general public and other interested parties the ability to comment on proposed land use cases being heard in their Local Government. It provides 24x7 access to your government organization and typically supplements statutory public notice requirements. In local governments that are using technology during their planning commission, zoning board of appeals, or governing body’s meetings, the online comments can be viewed during the hearing and at the same time residents are commenting in person on the proposal. If technology is not present, a manual report can be created and routed to appropriate staff.

Using the Application

The Land Use Public Comment application allows users to submit public comments on proposed development plans. When you hover over a proposed plan, you will see the Case Number, Case Name, and Hearing Date. If you then click on the plan, the application will display a popup window with the case details. The application has been configured to only show development plans with a future hearing date. It does not allow comments to be submitted on development plans with a hearing date in the past.

Finding an Area of Interest

There are three primary ways to identify an area of interest. You can interact directly with the map and click on a land use case when you've located it. You can also use your current location or an address to find a land use case near you. Finally, you can search for a land use case by name.

Map Based Navigation

There are several ways to interact with the map:
1. Hold down the shift key and drag a box on the map to zoom in
2. Hold Down Ctrl+Shift to zoom out
3. Roll your mouse roller to zoom in/out
4. Drag the zoom slider to zoom in/out

Searching

The search box can be used to find an address, or land use case you're interested in:

1. Use the radio dial to select the search method you'd like to use
2. Enter the address, or name of the land use case you're interested in
3. Click on the item in the list to locate the address or click on the case in the list

You can enter a partial address and then identify the correct value in the list.

Submitting Public Comments

Click on the proposed plan you're interested in and review the case information provided. The Additional Info tab contains any related documents. If appropriate, the actual design drawings are included. Finally, you can click on the Comments tab to submit a comment on the proposed plan. You can also review comments that have been submitted previously by other residents.

Click the Add Comment link in the information popup to add your own comment. A name, address, email or phone is required with each comment. When you're done entering your comment, click Submit.

Share Your Map

You can click on the Share Map button to share your map several different ways. You can compose an email, post to your Facebook wall or post a Tweet. When you use the Facebook or Twitter links, you'll be asked to sign in to your respective account.

More Information

The Public Comment application can be used by planning and zoning, building, or other local government organizations to deliver a web-based public comment application for your community. Please visit Esri's ArcGIS for Local Government Resource Center for additional information.

Browser Compatibility

The Land Use Public Comment application is supported on the following browsers: Microsoft IE 7/8/9, Mozilla Firefox, Google Chrome and Apple Safari.
